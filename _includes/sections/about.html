About

Intelligent Medical Systems Laboratory is led by Dr. Aleksei Tiulpin and conducts machine learning research to advance the field of AI for preventive care. We are headquartered at the Department of Radiology, Weill Cornell Medicine, New York City. Prior to that, the lab has been located at the University of Oulu, Finland.

Our expertise is in building machine learning methods and tools that advance diagnostics, treatment, and scientific discover across diseases, organs, and modalities.

We pursue three clinical aims:

Prevent disease
before it happens
Democratize
access to care
Prevent treatment
complications

From a machine learning standpoint, the key research efforts in the group are directed toward building methods that are

Scalable

Digest data from different modalities and with various sizes

Trustworthy

Can generate outputs that we, as humans, can trust.

Deployable

Align with the downstream goals
